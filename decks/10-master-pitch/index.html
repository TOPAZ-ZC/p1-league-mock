THE INCUMBENT IS BROKEN

P1 fills the vacuum.

PPA / UPA — Today's Broken Model

  • Governance Conflict. UPA replaced USA Pickleball as "independent" regulator — while owned by PPA/MLP.
  • Player Resentment. Delayed payments, one-sided contracts, minor-breach suspensions, culture of fear.
  • Closed Media. Fans banned from filming. Creators capped at 2 min/day — repeating F1's pre-2017 mistake.
  • U.S. Only. No professional international pathways, global governance, or media frameworks.

P1 — Built from the Opposite Philosophy

  • Open & Fan-First. Fans encouraged to film and share. Players become creators and storytellers.
  • Player-Aligned. Transparent governance. Athletes with real leverage. Credibility as competitive advantage.
  • Media-Native. Docuseries pipeline, daily content quotas, highlight syndication — cameras create the sport.
  • Truly Global. 20 events in 17 countries across Asia, Europe, Americas, Middle East from Year 1.
The Pattern That Compounds

F1 was stuck in this exact posture pre-2017. Liberty Media bought it, opened the media model, separated governance from commercial rights, and compounded to a $20B+ category. P1 starts there — not arrives there in ten years.
