01 · Selection Framework
PAGE 02
5 AXES · WEIGHTED SCORING

Every candidate city scored 1-5 on five axes. Weighted total drives qualification.

A city qualifies for Year-1 consideration when its weighted score is ≥ 3.8. The framework below is the model; the 8 launch cities that follow all clear the threshold with meaningful margin.

AXIS WEIGHT WHAT IT MEASURES
Pickleball TAM density 25% Active players per capita + projected 5-year growth inside the metro
Venue readiness 20% Arena capacity 4-8k, retractable-roof or court-surface compatibility, calendar availability in the target window
Media market reach 20% Domestic + international broadcast footprint; streaming concentration inside the metro
Political / regulatory tailwind 15% Tourism-board support, sports-hosting subsidies, visa / work-permit friction for visiting pros
Constructor alignment 20% Pre-existing brand gravity: hometown Constructor HQ, sponsor base, fan concentration
Why these five axes

TAM density drives the participation flywheel. Venue readiness drives execution risk. Media reach drives rights-value per stop. Regulatory tailwind drives cost structure. Constructor alignment drives local-market commercial activation. No axis is optional; a candidate failing any single axis below a 3 is disqualified regardless of weighted total.

Why the 4-swing shape

20 events is the institutional-league sweet spot — F1 runs 24, MotoGP 20, PGA 40+. The four-swing regional cluster matches tennis's Grand Slam arc, which is the most institutionally-understood racquet-sport structure. 5 stops per swing (4 standard + 1 Grand Slam) delivers monthly cadence inside each region while preserving festival-weekend density and player-travel sustainability. The Dubai Grand Slam is also the season-capping Tour Championship.

Hard Rock Stadium (retractable) or Miami-Dade Arena.
Media
TNT, ESPN+, DAZN footprint. International: LatAm spillover on Spanish-language feeds.
Constructor match
Finance, airline, apparel.
Austin, USA
STANDARD STOP
Why Austin
Fastest-growing US pickleball metro. Tech-money demographic. F1 US Grand Prix blueprint (COTA) proves the destination-sport format. Festival-weekend DNA already embedded — ACL Fest, SXSW.
Venue
Moody Center (UT).
Media
ESPN Austin + YouTube + Twitch streaming-native audience concentration.
Constructor match
Tech, beverage, footwear.
