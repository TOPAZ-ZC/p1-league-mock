08 · Dispute Resolution
PAGE 09
STEWARDSHIP · APPEAL · INDEPENDENCE GUARDRAILS

Four stages. Independent at each.

STAGE 1

On-Site Stewardship

Ramsports stewards at every event. Three stewards + three traveling refs. Real-time rulings on play, conduct, integrity flags. Published same-day.

STAGE 2

Integrity Office Review

Independent Ramsports integrity office reviews steward rulings on appeal. Drug-testing + betting-integrity pipeline feeds into review. Written findings.

STAGE 3

Ramsports Board Arbitration

Board-level appeal for sanctions above defined threshold (multi-event suspension, termination, monetary over $250k). Majority vote. Reasoned decision.

STAGE 4

External Arbitration

Final appeal to Court of Arbitration for Sport or equivalent neutral body on matters of process. Not available to overturn factual integrity findings.

Independence guardrails

What the Investor cannot do

  • Overrule a Ramsports integrity finding
  • Block a sanction for commercial reasons
  • Appoint or remove stewards / refs
  • Direct drug-testing scope or frequency
  • Review player appeals directly
Independence guardrails

What Constructors cannot do

  • Regulate own competition
  • Block sanctions against own players
  • Vote on integrity-office appointments
  • Direct drug-testing at home-stop events
  • Access confidential steward deliberations
The credibility dividend

Independent dispute resolution is priced directly into media-rights and sponsorship multiples. The F1 / FIA analog: disputes are resolved on the record, inside the sport, with external appeal only on process. Credibility compounds.
