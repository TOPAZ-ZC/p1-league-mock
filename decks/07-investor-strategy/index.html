THE PRIMARY ARCHETYPE

Sports infrastructure + participation + community thesis.

P1 is not pitched indiscriminately. The ideal anchor sits at the intersection of four theses: (a) sports-infrastructure investment at institutional scale, (b) participation-consumer category conviction, (c) community-experience / offline-time tailwind belief, and (d) media-rights compounding understanding. Investors holding all four already know how to read this deal; investors holding fewer will need education on the missing pillars.

Must-haves

Non-negotiable thesis alignment

  • Sports-infrastructure checkbook at $50M-$500M scale
  • Media-rights compounding track record
  • Multi-region (not US-only) operating posture
  • Multi-year horizon — tracking stock 2029-30, IPO 2030+
  • Governance-credibility premium understood as multiple driver
Nice-to-haves

Force-multipliers on the thesis

  • Existing participation-consumer portfolio (Athletic Brewing, Bandit, UGP, OLIPOP analogs)
  • Prior F1 / LIV / TKO / UFC exposure — pattern recognition
  • Festival / events operating experience
  • APAC or MENA balance-sheet breadth for Swing 3 / 4 activation
  • Co-investor syndicate capacity (up to 3 anchors at same terms)
The test

If the investor's portfolio already maps Angel City FC + Miami HEAT + Bandit Running + Athletic Brewing onto a single thesis, P1 is the next logical line in that portfolio. If not, the pitch needs to build the thesis before it asks for the check.

06 · 5-Part Conversation Framework
PAGE 07
EVERY INVESTOR CALL RUNS THIS ARC

Why YOU · Why P1 · Why now · Why this shape · Why $50M.

PART 01

Why YOU

Open with thesis-mapping. Don't pitch — mirror their portfolio language. Let them hear their own framework read back.

  • Reference 2-3 portfolio names by thesis fit
  • Name the pillar that P1 slots into
  • Invite the investor to connect the dots aloud
  • Goal: investor says "that's exactly what we're looking for"
PART 02

Why P1

The vacuum thesis. PPA / UPA is broken on four axes. P1 is built from the opposite philosophy on each.

  • Governance conflict → Ramsports independence
  • Player resentment → transparent governance
  • Closed media → open-media pipeline
  • U.S.-only → 20 stops / 17 markets from Year 1
PART 03

Why now

The Bartlett exponential. 75M model base → 300M scenario by 2032. Hybrid-work tailwind. Pre-institutional valuations.

  • Bartlett principle — exponential function
  • Hybrid work + AI giving back the afternoon
  • Category is pre-competitive on institutional side
  • Media-rights baseline will be set off Y1 pricing
PART 04

Why this shape

F1-style three-body architecture. Concorde + escrow + milestone = SPAC-like protection. $50M never at market risk until 8 Constructors sign.

  • Three-body separation (Investor / Ramsports / Constructors)
  • Escrow until milestone
  • Milestone-triggered release · full refund on miss
  • Liberty / F1 comp — $20B+ compounding
PART 05

Why $50M · why now

Binary ask: commit $50M to escrow before April 1, sign Concorde, become the commercial-rights holder of the category. OR wait and compete against RedBird / CVC / Liberty / Arctos doing this exact deal in Y2 at 3-4× pricing. Category-defining commercial rights are priced off Year 1 — not Year 3.

  • $50M → $250M+ implied → tracking stock 2029-30 → IPO $1B+
  • Board observer at close; voting at tracking-stock activation
  • Full refund if 8 Constructors not secured by Jul 1, 2026
  • Practical close date: March 15, 2026 for sign; Apr 1 Concorde formalization

Pacing notes
  • Intro → sign averages 8-12 weeks for primary-tier
  • Sovereign-tier runs 16-24 weeks; start in parallel
  • Do not send the REVISED deck before the intro call — it lands cold
  • Always close intro with explicit ask for follow-up window
Red-flag signals
  • Investor asks to see Constructor LOIs before signing — show, but watch for negotiation lever
  • Investor asks for exclusivity on $50M anchor — acceptable up to 30 days
  • Investor proposes revised valuation below $250M+ implied — flag to SLOTH immediately
  • Investor wants board veto on Ramsports — non-starter, exit the conversation
