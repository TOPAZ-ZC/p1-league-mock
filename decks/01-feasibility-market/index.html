Three rings. One defensible capture path.

Sized against the subset of the pickleball economy a global league can monetize — media rights, global sponsorship, gate & hospitality, licensing, and docuseries.

TAM · Total Addressable Market
$18-30B/yr

SAM · Serviceable Addressable Market
~$2.4B/yr
League-monetizable share inside Tier-1 cities (top 40 metros by active-player density). Media rights, global sponsorship, premium events, hospitality, licensing. The share a global league can realistically tap.
SOM · Serviceable Obtainable Market · Y5

SAM derivation

40 Tier-1 metros × ~750k active-player-equivalents × ~$80/yr league-attributable spend (ticketing, hospitality, subscription, merch) = ~$2.4B annual serviceable market. Expands as the 2032 player base grows 4×.

SOM derivation

04 · Macro Tailwinds
PAGE 05
THE STRUCTURAL SHIFT — NOT CYCLICAL

Time is being returned to consumers. They are spending it outdoors, competing.

Hybrid Work

40%+ afternoons

40%+ of US knowledge workers now have unstructured midday or afternoon hours — prime court-time windows that did not exist pre-2020.

AI Time Freedom

Discretionary hours compounding

As AI absorbs routine cognitive tasks, active leisure becomes the highest-value use of reclaimed hours. Participation sports are the structural beneficiary.

Participation Shift

Watch → Play

Consumers are migrating from passive watching to active play. Pickleball's 2-minute learnability makes that conversion instantaneous — unmatched by any other racquet sport.

The participation flywheel

Spectators become players. Players buy from Constructors. Constructors fund the next event. This flywheel is why P1 compounds differently than a traditional spectator-only league — every market that hosts an event grows its own player base, which grows its own audience, which grows its own commercial base.

Portfolio alignment

The macro thesis aligns directly with Sideline Group pillars — offline-time tailwind, community experiences, participation sports, sports infrastructure. P1 is the pure-play expression of all four at once.

Capital-markets context

Institutional sports capital (Arctos, RedBird, CVC, Silver Lake, PIF, Mubadala) is actively hunting the next category-defining asset. None has a pickleball position. P1 is the first and only way to build that position at Y1 pricing.

05 · The Bartlett Thesis
PAGE 06
EXPONENTIAL — NOT INCREMENTAL

Price it before the curve reflects it.

Albert Bartlett · University of Colorado

"The greatest shortcoming of the human race is our inability to understand the exponential function."

What the curve says

75M model → 300M by 2032

The 300M 2032 case is a scenario, not a guaranteed forecast. It is defensible only if U.S. growth converts into durable global adoption and court supply catches up across the 17-market calendar.

What the market will miss

Most valuations extrapolate linearly

Institutional comps — PPA/UPA, MLP, regional tours — are priced on trailing 12-month linear revenue. They underprice the category by 3-5×. The window to enter on linear-assumption pricing is 12-18 months.

Why before, not after

Waiting two years to confirm the exponential curve means buying at 3-4× today's implied valuation, with the same cash. The structural trade is to underwrite the curve now, at $250M+ implied — and hold through the tracking-stock activation at $1B+. Category-defining commercial rights are priced off the Y1 base rate.

06 · Risk Register
PAGE 07
IDENTIFIED EXECUTION RISKS + MITIGATIONS

Six material risks. Each one structurally mitigated.

Constructor recruitment
HIGH
8 global brands at $10M by July 1, 2026. Mitigation: 4 brands already in active term-sheet dialogue (April 2026). Sideline portfolio relationships unlock 2 additional co-investors. Concorde escrow = zero capital risk if milestone misses.
Regulatory / sanctioning
MEDIUM
USA Pickleball / UPA / IFP fragmentation; Olympic-name conflict risk. Mitigation: Ramsports is an independent governing body. Seymour Rifkind (WPF founder) chairs the Olympic-pathway relationship. No naming overlap.
Player-talent pool depth
MEDIUM
64 contracted pros across 8 Constructors; depth in top 200 is improving but thinner than tennis. Mitigation: $24M prize pool + team-feed economics attract cross-over athletes. Steve Kuhn (MLP founder, DUPR) on the Ramsports board runs the global rating pipeline.
Media-rights timing
MEDIUM
Y1 regional deals must convert to multi-year global packages by Y3. Mitigation: Launch-partner deal anchored in Y1 at global-highlight level; docuseries window Y2; unbundled live geography rights Y3-4. Modeled on F1 Liberty ramp.
Venue availability
LOW
4-8k-seat arenas across 17 markets on a 20-stop calendar. Mitigation: Venue inventory pre-validated (O2, Singapore Indoor Stadium, Coca-Cola Arena, Hard Rock, WiZink, Moody, Ariake, Qudos). Ramsports + local promoter model runs load-in/operate/teardown on a 4-day cycle.
Olympic calendar conflict
LOW
2028 LA Games could bid for pickleball inclusion; potential cannibalization of pro calendar. Mitigation: Olympic pathway flows through WPF (Rifkind) and would accelerate P1 TAM, not compete. Commercial P1 entity and Olympic pathway are orthogonal.
