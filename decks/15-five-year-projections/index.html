01 · Methodology
PAGE 02
HOW THESE NUMBERS WERE BUILT

Bottom-up, cross-checked against comparable-scale comps.

Every line traced to a source: event-level unit economics, comparable-sport media-rights ramps (F1, PGA, LIV, MLP), and sponsorship inventory valued per category. Where the public pitch deck implies a bull case, this pro forma uses the base case.

HARD REVENUE

Contracted, billed, collected

Media rights deals signed. Sponsorship inventory sold. Gate + hospitality run through event P&L. Constructor team-feed pulled from Constructor license. Licensing + merchandise net of COGS + royalty splits. Docuseries rights paid.

MEDIA VALUE (EARNED)

Non-cash brand halo

Earned impressions × category CPM. Tracks what a sponsor or Constructor would have paid to buy the same exposure on open markets. Reported separately from hard revenue — this is what the brand gets, not what the league collects.

HAIRCUT VS PITCH DECK

~15% conservative

Pitch deck Y5 league revenue: $210M. This pro forma: $184M base case (12% haircut). Pitch deck Constructor Y5: $13.1M. This pro forma: $10.7M (18% haircut). Gives the President honest floor, not ceiling.

CPM ASSUMPTIONS

$15-$20 blended

Blended against: linear sports $25-35 CPM, streaming mid-roll $12-18, social clip $4-8. Weighted toward social distribution given open-media model. Conservative vs F1's $45-65 CPM on premium markets.

MEDIA-RIGHTS EXPOSURE

Highest single-line sensitivity

Every 10% miss on media rights drops Y5 EBITDA by ~$7M and compresses Investor MOIC by ~25 bps. Mitigation: multi-year regional deals locked by Y2 close (creates floor).

CONSTRUCTOR RETENTION

Renewal at Y5 matters more than defaults in Y3

Y5 Constructor re-auction value is where ~40% of investor MOIC lives. 7-of-8 retention is the base; 5-of-8 drops to 2.3× Investor MOIC. Structure Y5 renewal terms carefully.

The institutional takeaway

These numbers are the floor, not the ceiling. Bull cases exist. Bear case is capped at the escrow refund. The President's question for any investor should be: "Does your fund mandate price a 3.3×-5.6× at 27-41% IRR on a 5-year hold with a zero-loss floor?"
