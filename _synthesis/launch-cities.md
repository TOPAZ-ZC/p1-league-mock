# P1 World Tour — Launch Cities & 20-Stop Calendar (canonical)

**The city-selection framework + Year-1 calendar. Every deck pulls geography + city rationale from here.**

---

## 1. Selection framework — 5 axes

Each candidate city scored 1-5 on five axes, weighted:

| Axis | Weight | What it measures |
|---|---:|---|
| **Pickleball TAM density** | 25% | Active players per capita + projected 5-yr growth |
| **Venue readiness** | 20% | Arena capacity 4-8k, retractable roof or court-surface compatibility, date availability |
| **Media market reach** | 20% | Domestic + international broadcast footprint; streaming concentration |
| **Political / regulatory tailwind** | 15% | Tourism boards, sports-hosting subsidies, visa/work-permit friction for visiting pros |
| **Constructor alignment** | 20% | Pre-existing brand gravity (hometown Constructor HQ, sponsor base, fan concentration) |

Cities scoring ≥3.8 weighted qualify for Year-1 launch consideration.

## 2. The 8 Year-1 Launch Cities

Recommendation across 4 quarterly swings, with Grand Slam anchors in each.

### Swing 1 — AMERICAS (Q1 2027, February–April)

**1. MIAMI, USA** — Grand Slam anchor (Swing 1)
- **Why:** Highest pickleball density per capita in the US. Miami-Dade has ~280k active players (2026 est). Hard Rock Stadium + FTX Arena venue optionality. Miami as global sports hub post-F1 GP, Formula E, ATP Masters 1000. Mazlin/Sideline HQ resonance.
- **Venue:** Hard Rock Stadium (retractable) or Miami-Dade Arena.
- **Media:** TNT, ESPN+, DAZN footprint. International: Latin America spillover.
- **Constructor match:** Finance / Airline / Apparel.

**2. AUSTIN, USA**
- **Why:** Fastest-growing US pickleball metro. Tech-money demographic. F1 US Grand Prix blueprint (COTA). Festival-weekend DNA (ACL Fest, SXSW).
- **Venue:** Moody Center (UT).
- **Media:** ESPN Austin, plus YouTube + Twitch streaming-native audience.
- **Constructor match:** Tech, Beverage, Footwear.

### Swing 2 — EUROPE (Q2 2027, May–July)

**3. LONDON, UK** — Grand Slam anchor (Swing 2)
- **Why:** Europe's largest pickleball growth market (3× YoY 2024-2026). Wimbledon halo. Global media capital. 34M active English-speaking sports fans.
- **Venue:** O2 Arena or Copper Box (2012 Olympic legacy).
- **Media:** Sky Sports, BBC Sport, Eurosport. Global English-language feed.
- **Constructor match:** Finance (JPMorgan, Morgan Stanley), Apparel (adidas), Airline (British Airways).

**4. MADRID, SPAIN**
- **Why:** Fastest-growing European pickleball country behind UK. Padel crossover (Spain has world's densest padel infrastructure). Madrid Open tennis legacy.
- **Venue:** WiZink Center (Palacio Vistalegre secondary).
- **Media:** Movistar +, Eurosport Iberia.
- **Constructor match:** Airline (Iberia), Beverage, Footwear.

### Swing 3 — ASIA-PACIFIC (Q3 2027, August–October)

**5. SINGAPORE** — Grand Slam anchor (Swing 3)
- **Why:** Asia-Pacific financial + media hub. Singapore Sports Hub purpose-built for international league play. Government sports subsidy program. Time zone bridge for APAC + MENA broadcast.
- **Venue:** Singapore Indoor Stadium.
- **Media:** Mediacorp, beIN Sports Asia, global English feed via Sky.
- **Constructor match:** Airline (Singapore Airlines), Finance (DBS, UBS), Tech.

**6. TOKYO, JAPAN**
- **Why:** Pickleball is #1 YoY-growth urban-participation sport in Japan (2024-2026). Olympic venue inventory. Unique cultural crossover with soft-racket heritage (tennis, badminton).
- **Venue:** Ariake Arena (Tokyo 2020 legacy).
- **Media:** DAZN Japan, NHK Sports, TV Tokyo.
- **Constructor match:** Apparel (ASICS, Mizuno), Tech (Sony), Beverage (Asahi, Suntory).

**7. SYDNEY, AUSTRALIA**
- **Why:** English-speaking APAC. Summer-flipped calendar opens Q3 AU-summer date. Racquet-sport culture (tennis + squash). Media reach into NZ + Pacific.
- **Venue:** Qudos Bank Arena.
- **Media:** Fox Sports Australia, Stan Sport. ATP/WTA comp analog.
- **Constructor match:** Airline (Qantas), Finance (ANZ), Apparel.

### Swing 4 — MIDDLE EAST + GRAND FINALS (Q4 2027, November–January)

**8. DUBAI, UAE** — Grand Slam anchor (Swing 4 / Tour Championship)
- **Why:** Sports-hosting capital of MENA. Government sports investment (Dubai Sports Council). Emirates as title sponsor candidate. Tourism + hospitality multiplier. Tax-friendly prize-money jurisdiction (critical for player retention across the Tour Championship).
- **Venue:** Coca-Cola Arena (17k) or Hamdan Sports Complex.
- **Media:** Abu Dhabi Sports / beIN Sports MENA. DAZN MENA.
- **Constructor match:** Airline (Emirates, Qatar, Etihad), Finance (Mubadala, ADQ), Apparel.

## 5. Festival-weekend anatomy (5-day stop)

| Day | Activity |
|---|---|
| **Thursday** | Load-in. Celebrity / pro-am invitational. Fan-access practice. |
| **Friday** | Celebrity doubles (fundraiser). Opening concert. Qualifying brackets. |
| **Saturday** | Singles + doubles semis. Music headliner night. Hospitality peak. |
| **Sunday** | Finals day. Trophy presentation. Constructor brand showcases. |
| **Monday** | Tear-down. Press cycle. Highlight package drop for streaming. |

**Each stop targets:**
- 4-5k ticketed weekend attendance
- $1-2M gate + hospitality
- 200-400M media impressions (live + highlight + docuseries residual)
- 3-4 Constructor brand activations per stop
